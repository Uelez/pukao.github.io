HOW TO BUY

Want a Lambo? Follow the steps below:

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet
Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list.
Buy and send BUSD to MetaMask
Buy BUSD on an exchange (Coinbase, Binance, etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. Be sure that is starts with an "0x"
Go to PancakeSwap and swap for $PUKAO
Navigate to PancakeSwap and select PUKAO. Be sure to use a slippage tolerance of 11% or more. Demand might require a higher tolerance for the transaction to complete.
Confirm purchase and HODL!
Once the transaction is complete, add $PUKAO to your MetaMask or TrustWallet. Finally and most importantly, HODL!
